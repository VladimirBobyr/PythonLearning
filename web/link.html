Date: 11-Dec-2019

Group Company: Deutsche Börse Services sro

DevOps Engineer in Regulatory (f/m/d) 

Full-time | Permanent

 

Deutsche Börse Group (DBG) is one of the largest exchange organizations and market infrastructure providers worldwide – and information technology is our backbone. Our business is mainly transaction and data-driven, and is powered by an incredible amount of incoming real-world financial market data every day. Our software engineers engage in an array of fields, from latency-optimization (nano-seconds) of our trading applications, to risk and margin calculator algorithms, expansion of data APIs on our digital business platform, and machine learning and natural language processing. If you strive to take ownership and develop creative solutions, are fascinated by technology, and like to work in a challenging and fast paced environment – then you are exactly the person we are looking for.

 

We are building a new team which is end-to-end (Build, Run) responsible for Regulatory Reporting Hub. The team build the application from scratch with core functions in data ingest, data stream, data store, reporting & analytics, as well as core regulatory relevant functions and features.

Tasks/responsibilities

  • Design and implement automated infrastructure and application deployments
  • Be part of the whole development process from development to production
  • Build native data processing / data flow features in a secure and test-driven manner
  • Work closely with developers and architects to design new systems

 

Qualifications/required skills

  • BA or BSC in Science or Math or Computer Science, preference for MSc in Computer science, Math or Engineering
  • Scripting languages: Bash, Python
  • Experience with Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, Ansible, Jenkins, GitHub
  • Experience with at least one cloud platforms: Azure, AWS, GCP
  • Experience with agile development practices and methods, e.g. Scrum, Kanban
  • Very good written and spoken English skills

 

Desirable skills

  • Practical experiences with: Streams architecture, Multithreading, TCP/IP and Middleware Technologies (Kafka, FIX, AMQP)
  • Programming competency: Golang, Java
  • Experience with Helm, Istio, Sonar, Fortify
  • Big Data technologies: Apache Spark, Hadoop, HBase, Cloudera, Impala
  • NoSQL: Redis, Cassandra, Mongo
  • Experience with Microservices architecture
  • Background in system design, web infrastructure and scalable cloud systems

 


Dedication, team and communication skills, flexibility as well as competent handling of MS Office applications round out your profile. There are numerous good reasons to work for us: responsibility at an early stage, attractive social benefits, an international working environment and a broad variety of career opportunities. Applications from disabled persons are welcome.

Are you interested in working with a pleasant and very dedicated team? Convince us with an appealing application. Please use our online application portal.


Deutsche Börse Group, Human Resources
